Priority Scheduling
•A priority number (integer) is associated with each process
•Scheduler ALWAYS  choose a process of high priority over one of 
lower priority
•Smaller number == higher priority
•Still suffers from starvation , where low priority may never 
execute. However , aging can be implemented to increase the 
priority of a  process based on its age or execution history
Preemptive Priority
Executing job is pre-empted if its 
priority is lower than priority of 
new job. If equal, use FCFS.Nonpreemptive Priority
When CPU is available, it is 
allocated to the job with highest 
priority. If equal, use FCFS.
