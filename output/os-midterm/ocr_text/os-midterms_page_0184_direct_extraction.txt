Key Scheduling Terms
•Arrival Time (AT)  - The time when a process enters the ready queue  
(i.e., becomes ready for execution).
•Burst Time (BT)  / CPU Burst Time  - The total time  the process needs on 
the CPU to complete its execution.
•Start Time (ST)  - The time when the process actually starts  executing on 
the CPU for the first time.
•Finish Time (FT)  / Completion Time (CT)  - The time when the process 
completes all its execution  (both CPU and I/O if applicable).Process Arrival
